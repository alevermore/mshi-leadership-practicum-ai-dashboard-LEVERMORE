## Practicium Context: 
##       My practicum site is Morehouse School of Medicine under my supervisor, Dr. Christina Muhammad. This organization is committed to health equity, community engagement, and mentorship to future health professionals.
      
## Leadership Question:
##       Can Morehouse School of Medicine utilize leadership skills to tackle the ongoing crisis of prevalent chronic illnesses by utilizing a subset of the risk factors to accurately predict whether an individual has diabetes? 
      
## Data Plan:
##       This project uses a simulated data set from Kaggle using the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System. A CSV dataset from 2015 was used, containing responses from Americans on health-related risk behaviors, chronic health conditions, and preventative services usage. 
      
 # Health Informatics Leadership Impact Dashboard
