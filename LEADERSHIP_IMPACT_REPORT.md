# Leadership Impact Report

## Introduction & Practicum Context
This project uses public health data to explore leadership strategies for chronic disease prevention and community health improvement, focusing on diabetes risk and lifestyle factors.

## Leadership Question
How can health informatics leadership use population health data to improve diabetes prevention, engagement, and long-term health outcomes?

## Data & Methods
- Dataset: BRFSS diabetes health indicators dataset
- Type: Public health behavioral data
- Tools: Python, Excel, GitHub, AI tools
- Methods: Descriptive analytics and AI-assisted interpretation

## Key Findings
- Prevalence of diabetes risk indicators
- Lifestyle behavior patterns
- Physical activity gaps
- Health perception trends

## Leadership Analysis
This data highlights opportunities for leadership interventions in education, preventive care, community engagement, and health equity.

## Action Plan
1. Community education programs
2. Digital engagement strategies
3. Preventive screening initiatives
4. Health literacy campaigns
5. Data-driven outreach targeting

## Reflection on Leadership Growth
This project strengthened my understanding of data-informed leadership, ethical AI use, and the role of informatics in population health management.
